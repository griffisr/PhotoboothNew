How Do I Book a Photo Booth?

Renting one of our Michigan photo booths is an extremely simple 5 step process.

1) Check Booth Availability

Submit a check availability request to make sure we have a booth available for rental on your date. Even if you’re early in the planning stages of your event, we suggest you send your request in right away because our 9 booths regularly book months in advance.

2) Choose Your Style & Package

Check out our booth types to choose between an Open-Air or Curtain Style photo booth. Would you prefer 2x6” photo strips, or full sized 4x6” images? Once you’ve chosen a style, select one of our popular packages, or call us to discuss creating a custom photo booth solution.

3) Reserve Your Photo Booth

Once you’ve decided on a specific package, we’ll put all the details in writing and you can reserve your booth with a deposit and signed contract.

4) Customize Options

Once your reservation is received, we’ll work on all of the customizations for your event such as designing a personalized logo for your prints, or a custom start screen for the photo booth. Optionally, you can also upgrade to a branded red carpet style fabric backdrop.

5) Enjoy!
